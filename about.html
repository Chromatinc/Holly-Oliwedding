A Quick Note for Our U.S. Guests

If you're heading to the the UK from elsewhere, you'll need a UK ETA (Electronic Travel Authorisation) before your trip.
Don't worry, it's a simple online application that only takes a few minutes and costs £20 per person. Please make sure to arrange this before you travel to the UK.
